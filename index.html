Starting my career in 1988, as a receptionist, working my way through as a deal secretary, administrator and then an Executive Office Administrator for a large downtown Royal Lepage Brokerage in Toronto, allowed me to spend time with my two children growing up, participating in their schooling, and their organized sport events.
